Emergency Response Timeline

Our automated response system ensures rapid action based on hazard severity

0-15 min
Critical Hazards
Immediate SMS alerts to all registered users, emergency services notification, and automated warning systems activation.
15-60 min
High Severity
Local authority coordination, community leaders notification, and preparation of emergency response teams.
Medium Severity
Advisory alerts, monitoring station activation, and preparation of contingency plans for affected areas.
Low Severity
General awareness notifications and increased monitoring of the affected coastal region.
